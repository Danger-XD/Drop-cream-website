About Drop Cream & Co.

Welcome to Drop Cream & Co., where we create delightful ice cream experiences for everyone. Located in the heart of Dhaka, we are dedicated to serving you the finest and most delicious ice cream in town.

Our Story

Drop Cream & Co. was founded with a passion for crafting the best ice cream using the freshest ingredients. Our journey began in 2023 with a small shop in Gulshan-2, Dhaka. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved destination for ice cream lovers of all ages, known for our unique flavors and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to bring joy and happiness through the magic of ice cream. We believe in using high-quality, locally-sourced ingredients to create ice cream that not only tastes great but also supports our community. Whether it's a hot summer day or a special celebration, Drop Cream & Co. is here to make every moment sweeter.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Quality Ingredients: We use only the best ingredients to ensure every scoop is bursting with flavor.
  • Unique Flavors: From classic favorites to innovative creations, our menu has something for everyone.
  • Community Focused: We are committed to giving back to the community and supporting local suppliers.
  • Customer Experience: Our friendly staff and inviting atmosphere make every visit a memorable one.
